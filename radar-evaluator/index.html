Weighted Score vs. Radar Area — Bauhaus Edition

Weighted Score Method
Stotal = Σ(Scorei × Wi) ÷ 9.0
Prioritizes high-weight dimensions. Favors teams with strong Tech & Innovation.
S: Tech 2.0 S: Innovation 2.0 A: Team 1.5 A: Ambition 1.5 B: Market 1.0 B: Commercial 1.0
Radar Area Method
A = ½ × Σ(xiyi+1 − xi+1yi)
Measures polygon area via shoelace formula. Rewards balanced capability profiles.
Key Insight: Four dimensions reveal different team strengths: Weighted Score (strategic focus) | Radar Area (overall coverage) | Average Score (general capability) | Balance (consistency across dimensions).
📊 Balance Score: Converted from standard deviation to 0-100% scale. Higher = more balanced across all dimensions.
Balance = (1 - σ/σₘₐₓ) × 100%, where σ = √[Σ(xᵢ - μ)² / n], σₘₐₓ = 50
